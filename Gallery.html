Sagara

Sagara is a hardware device designed to ensure that crab cultivation operates smoothly without interference from external sources. This device not only controls an electric motor to maintain the pH level of the water in the crab farming system but also manages and maintains the crabs' needs, including food and medication. With a system that can be controlled both automatically and manually via the internet, crab cultivation becomes easier and more efficient.
